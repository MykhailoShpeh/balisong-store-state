Introducing the Moab x Meridian - the debut build on our all-new trainer platform. For the first iteration of the Moab, we took our legendary Meridian scales, honed them from solid 7075 aluminum, and added a touch of Moab flair.

  • Built for durability
    Engineered to endure the demands of daily flipping, the Moab x Meridian combines 7075 aluminum handles and spacers with a hardened trainer blade for exceptional toughness. An extended tang provides drop protection to the pivot area, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability.
  • Modular Weight System
    Easily switch between 10 different weight configurations - from neutral to very handle-heavy - all without the need for disassembly.
  • Pivots 2.0
    Redesigned with upgraded materials and a finer thread, our new pivots are built for durability and are nearly impossible to strip.
  • Glidr Tune
    The Moab x Meridian is dialed with that unmistakable Glidr Tune - featuring hand-tuned bushings for no tap and minimal play, delivering a solid, reliable setup right out of the box.
